My Passion


Lifting weights is an almost daily routine of mine. I wake up at 6:45 in the morning every day (except for Sundays) so I can fit gym time into my schedule. I have been lifting weights for almost 5 years and have seen significant progress in my physical and mental health. There is so much to learn about weightlifting: proper form, technique, understanding and pushing past your limits, proper diet, sleep, etc. A couple of things that have helped me learn all I can about weightlifting are videos, recording workouts, and understanding the benefits of working out.

Training Videos


One thing that really helped me get started was watching training videos on YouTube. My favorite science-based lifter is Jeff Nippard. He has a lot of great training videos for beginners to advanced level lifters. One video I want to link is how to advance during all stages of lifting. I love this very informative video.

Recording Your Lifts


The most important thing you can do to improve is to track and record your lifts. My wife is my gym partner, and she always records the weight she lifts in a note on her phone. She challenged me to start recording my lifts, and my strength has increased significantly. I realized I got into a routine of lifting the same weights and doing the same workouts, and my progress was plateauing. Benefits


My quality of life has increased since I started lifting weights. Research has shown that there are so many benefits to lifting weights. Some of those benefits include:
  1. Increase Strength
  2. Reduces Risk of Chronic Disease
    • Heart Disease
    • Diabetes
    • Lung Disease
  3. Reduces Stress
  4. Boosts Energy
  5. Inspiration to change your life
